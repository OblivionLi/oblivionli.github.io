Hi, my name is Liviu (24 yo - 2021) and I'm a Web Developer building web apps and plugins/games as a side hobby. I've started playing around with programming languages 3 years ago (~2017-2018), mainly with Java, but started building apps "professionally" 2 years ago. By "professionally" I mean self-projects and here and there projects for my friends.

All of my current programming knowledge started when I was a kid (~12-14 yo) playing a fps game (Counter-Strike) on zombie community servers. Even though this doesn't tell much, those servers were full of cool plugins made by the community that lifted the enjoyment tremendously. I still remember playing a lot when I was back home from school with my friends. But with everything that's fun, there comes a time for drama and because of it, me and my friends started learning Pawn programming language to script/edit existing plugins for that gamemode and built a few community servers on our own.

Later on, after I finished high school I knew that programming will be my future and my life job, I still don't see myself work in another field than development. My passion for programming is the feeling of just building something that others will use, or the relief after tackling a hard problem after a few days, it's like a "dopamine dose". Even though it can be a stressful and nerve wracking job, the enjoyment when you finally solve said problem its overwhelming.

Even though right now I'm focussed on web development, I consider myself a developer in general and always looking to build anything that seems cool; I'm not locked on a specific branch in the dev environment, I'm like a sponge, as long as I can still learn new things, no matter what type of programming there are out there (software/web/game etc..) I'm still interested and still learning.

At the moment I build web apps using PHP (mainly Laravel) with React (& Redux for state management) as my primary programming languages.

This is just a summary and for the sake of not writing a novel, I will keep it short. If you have any question or want to hire me, send me an email.
